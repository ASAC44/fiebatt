# Fiebatt Privacy Policy

Fiebatt processes account information, uploaded media, editing instructions, generated media, and operational metadata to provide the video-editing service.

Generation providers are configured by Fiebatt on the server. Users are never asked to supply provider API keys, and provider credentials are never exposed through the web app or Codex tools. Uploaded and generated media remain private and are exposed through time-limited URLs.

Users may remove projects and generated media. Service logs must redact platform credentials, OAuth tokens, and signed storage URLs.

Questions or deletion requests may be filed through the repository issue tracker until a dedicated privacy contact is published.
