# Fiebatt Privacy Policy

Fiebatt processes account information, uploaded media, editing instructions, generated media, and operational metadata to provide the video-editing service.

Provider credentials supplied through Fiebatt settings are encrypted at rest, used only to perform requests initiated by the account owner, and are never returned through Codex tools. Uploaded and generated media remain private and are exposed through time-limited URLs.

Users may remove projects, generated media, and stored provider credentials. Service logs must redact credentials, OAuth tokens, and signed storage URLs.

Questions or deletion requests may be filed through the repository issue tracker until a dedicated privacy contact is published.
